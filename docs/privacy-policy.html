Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tidbit ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our SMS-based task management service (the "Service"). By using the Service, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our SMS Service:

  • Phone Number: Your mobile phone number, which is required to send and receive SMS messages through the Service.
  • Message Content: The content of SMS messages you send to and receive from the Service, including task management commands and responses.
  • Message Metadata: Timestamps, message identifiers, and delivery status associated with each SMS exchange.
  • Task Data: Information related to your task management activities, including task status, notes, completion dates, and scheduling preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver daily task reminders and weekly review summaries via SMS.
  • Process your SMS commands (such as completing, skipping, or rescheduling tasks).
  • Generate AI-powered responses to your messages to facilitate task management.
  • Maintain conversation history for service continuity and message deduplication.
  • Monitor and improve the performance and reliability of the Service.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate the Service:

  • Twilio: We use Twilio to send and receive SMS messages. Your phone number and message content are transmitted through Twilio's platform. Twilio's privacy policy is available at https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy.
  • Anthropic: We use Anthropic's Claude AI to process your messages and generate responses. Message content is sent to Anthropic for natural language processing. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at https://www.anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Railway: We use Railway for application hosting and data storage. Railway's privacy policy is available at https://railway.app/legal/privacy.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Conversation logs and task data are stored for the duration of your active plan. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Cryptographic validation of incoming SMS messages to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Authentication requirements for administrative access to the Service.
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse of the Service.
  • Secure transmission of data between the Service and third-party providers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt Out: You may stop receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message from the Service.
  • Access Your Data: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Delete Your Data: You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
  • Get Help: You may text HELP to receive assistance and contact information.

7. No Sale of Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We do not share your opt-in data or consent status with any third parties except as required to provide the Service as described above.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via SMS before the changes take effect. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
